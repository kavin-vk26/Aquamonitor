About AquaMonitor

Advanced AI-powered water quality monitoring for aquaculture

Our Technology

AquaMonitor utilizes state-of-the-art LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) neural networks to predict water quality parameters with unprecedented accuracy. Our universal model is trained on extensive historical weather and water quality data, enabling reliable predictions for aquaculture operations worldwide.

Key Capabilities

Real-Time Monitoring

Continuous assessment of critical water quality parameters including temperature, dissolved oxygen, and pH levels.

Predictive Analytics

LSTM-based forecasting provides advance warning of potential water quality issues before they impact your stock.

Species Optimization

Customized thresholds and recommendations for 8+ species including tilapia, salmon, catfish, shrimp, and more.

Global Coverage

Universal model supports operations anywhere in the world with location-based climate adaptation.

Technical Specifications

  • Model Architecture: LSTM with 64 units, dropout regularization
  • Training Data: 365 days of historical weather and water quality data
  • Prediction Horizon: Next hour forecasting with 24-hour sequence input
  • Parameters Monitored: Water temperature, dissolved oxygen, pH level
  • Update Frequency: Real-time with hourly predictions
  • Supported Species: General fish, tilapia, catfish, salmon, trout, carp, shrimp, prawn

How It Works

  1. Data Collection: System gathers weather data (temperature, humidity, precipitation, wind) from global meteorological sources
  2. Parameter Calculation: Universal formulas compute water quality parameters based on climate-agnostic principles
  3. LSTM Prediction: Neural network analyzes 24-hour sequences to forecast next-hour conditions
  4. Risk Assessment: Species-specific thresholds evaluate fish health risks and generate recommendations
  5. Visualization: Interactive charts and reports present data in actionable formats
